Our Partners

When your company partners with Rosie’s Southside Animal Shelter, it is a win-win situation! That’s because being a good corporate citizen is also a good business decision. Because we don’t receive local, state or federal tax dollars, we rely on these donations to make our work possible.Companies that sponsor Southside receive great visibility some of which include recognition on our website, Facebook page and in our email newsletters. Contact rosie@dogandcatshelter.com for more information.

Here are some companies that support us:

  • PetSmart Charities
  • Kroger
  • Petco
  • Amazon Smile
