About Us

Who Are We?

We are an animal shelter that specializes in dogs and cats. We have srved Fort Wayne for over 20 years. We find forver homes for thousands of pets each year. We care for our animals and do our best to place them in a home that suits them. Our dogs and cats require a lot of attention, some more than others. We provide medical and rehabilitation services, enrichment, and behavior assessments as necessary to give our animals the best opportunity for adoption. Every dog and cat available for adoption is spayed or neutered and current on all vaccines.
